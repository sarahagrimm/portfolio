About Me

I'm currently a student / intern at Geekwise Academy in Fresno, California and also a student studying at West Hills College Lemoore. I've had a passion for computers, games, and technology in general since I was a kid. You could always find me playing a video game, messing with settings on the family computer, or taking something electronic apart and trying to figure out how to put it back together.

I first fell in love with coding and design at the age of 12, when I found MySpace and started learning how to use HTML to create a profile that was unique to me. For years, I was lost in determining what I wanted to do as a career, but kept coming back to coding with little to no idea what I could do with it. That was until Bitwise Industries partnered with my school and invited us to participate in their amazing program. Now I've found my dream job. Development isn't just something I do for a job, It's something I love. If you hire me, you'll find that I add a little bit of something that not all web developers can offer, passion.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy listening to music, playing video games, watching TV, and playing with my dog.
